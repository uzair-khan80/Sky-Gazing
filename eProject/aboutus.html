Welcome To Our Website

Sky gazing involves observing the night sky for celestial phenomena. To enhance the experience, choose a dark location away from city lights, consider the moon phase for optimal visibility, and use tools like star maps, mobile apps, binoculars, or telescopes. Learn to identify constellations, be patient, check the weather, and dress appropriately for the night. Enjoy the tranquility of a quiet spot, and keep an eye out for special astronomical events such as meteor showers and eclipses, allowing time for your eyes to adjust and fully appreciate the beauty of the cosmos.

The astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects such as stars, planets, comets, and galaxies, as well as the phenomena that occur outside Earth's atmosphere.
